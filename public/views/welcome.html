Apply to the 1-week bootcamp

Welcome to the admission process of the 1-week bootcamp. We’re glad to know that you want to learn how to exploit the full potential of JavaScript and AngularJS. As stated, this bootcamp is for developers who master at least one programming language other than JavaScript (C#, Java Go, Python, PHP …).
From application to decision, the whole process might take up to 2 weeks. So the earlier you apply, the better.
You can find more information about the program on our website.


Steps for the admission process

  1. Registration: You submit your application.
  2. In person Interview: Once your application submitted, we will come back to you to set up an In person interview (skype/hangout/appearin, …).
    During the interview, we will assess your level which will in turn help us to determine if you’re eligible for the bootcamp and if your skills matches those of the other participants.
  3. Decision: We deliver an admission decision to you, and talk about next-steps.
